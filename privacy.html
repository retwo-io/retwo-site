Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

ReTwo LLC ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data across our applications, including Vanta and Ledger.

1. Data Collection

We believe your data belongs to you. Our applications are designed to be "Stateless." We do not host your media or financial files. Connection data to your personal servers (e.g., Jellyfin) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

Our apps may connect to third-party APIs (such as TMDB for metadata or Real-Debrid for cloud storage) at the user's request. Your credentials for these services are stored securely in the system Keychain.

3. Analytics

We may use anonymous telemetry to improve application performance. This data contains no personally identifiable information (PII).

4. Contact

For questions regarding privacy, contact us at legal@retwo.io
